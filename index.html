Cassandra Cardiff Yoga

Oxford, Oxfordshire · Group and private yoga classes · ccardiff@dal.ca

I am an experienced yoga teacher with 1000+ teaching hours obtained in Canada, South Korea and the United Kingdom. Trained to teach Baptiste-Inspired Power Flow, Core Strength Vinyasa and Yoga For All, my classes emphasise alignment, accessibility, strength building and compassionate self-exploration. My focus is on playfully adjusting postures and flows to meet the needs and body of the yoga practitioner - not the other way around.

My Classes

My Training

Core Strength

Vinyasa

This perspective on yoga, created by anatomy expert Sadie Nardini, consists of intelligently cued postures and sequences that bring you into efficient, safe and stable core-generated alignment. Core Strength Vinyasa simplifies accessing and comprehending the “core” while helping you to cultivate strength, flexibility, mindfulness and confidence. Because of its multi-level actions and focus on constant core connection, Core Strength Vinyasa is one of the most effective yoga practices for sparking major changes both inside and out.

Yoga For All

Accessible Hatha

Yoga For All is a style designed to be accessible, fun and rewarding to each and every student, regardless of body shape, size or ability. In this style, we explore how yoga and asana work in your individual body with the use of props, breath work and compassionate self-exploration. Our aim is personal empowerment and celebrating what our bodies - regardless of what they look like - can do both on the mat and off of it.

Baptiste-Inspired

Power Flow

Inspired by the methods of globally renowned yoga instructor Baron Baptiste, this power flow sequence is simultaneously vigorous, calming and playful. It will challenge you to build internal heat, strength and new edges of possibility while teaching you to value who you are both on the mat and off of it. Although fast-paced and dynamic, options and modifications are offered throughout to suit your individual body and practice.
